Hello my name is Geneva Faraci. I am an anthropology major, and a web programing and honors minor. I plan on pursuing my research in web anthropology and work mainly in the middle east.
Enrolled at Ithaca College, I will graduate from there in May 2016. I graduated from a non-traditional highschool, Francis W. Parker Charter Essential School, where I actively pursued theater. I currently work at ITS Helpdesk at Ithaca College, and as an intern in our Marketing and Communications department creating content for our social media sites. I spent this past summer working at MITRE as a user design co-op. In my spare time I love to sing and work on my photography. You can see a variety of my work on the portfolio page.
