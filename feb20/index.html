The Use of Social Media during the February 20th Movement

The 20th of February Movement in Morocco was just one political effort made throughout the entirety of the Arab Spring. Social media played an important role not only in the Arab Spring but in the 20th February Movement.


Methods

Three main methods were used to gather information:

  1. Interviews
  2. Participant-observation of social networking sites
  3. Literary review of scholarly works and news articles
